This is an interview study which will take approximately 45 minutes to complete, in which we will ask you to respond to several examples of hypothetical "future apps" that might be made to help you protect your privacy in the future.

If you volunteer and you will be asked to fill in a simple (10 min) demographic questionnaire online. Based on responses to the questionnaire, we will select 15 participants to follow up for a 30-45 min chat, either in person or over Skype about the hypothetical apps we are designing.

If you are selected and complete the interview, you will be given an Amazon gift certificate of £10 for your time. For those who are not selected for interview, we will enter your participant IDs into a raffle for 3 amazon gift certificates (valued each £10) for completing the online questionnaire.

This study collection is purely anonymous and no personally identifying information will kept about you. (For scheduling purposes, an e-mail address will be asked for below; this e-mail will be kept separate from your data and destroyed after the interview).
