lieCloud: Understanding Perceptions of Privacy-Preserving Disinformation Strategies

a study by Max Van Kleek, Dave Murray-Rust, Kieron O'Hara, and Sir Nigel Shadbolt.

This is an interview study which will take approximately 45 minutes to complete, in which we will ask you to respond to several examples of hypothetical "future apps" that might be made to help you protect your privacy in the future.

If you volunteer and you will be asked to fill in a simple demographic questionnaire online, and then to chat with one of the researchers above either in person or over Skype about the apps we will describe to you. If you complete the study we will gladly reimburse you an amazon.co.uk gift certificate worth £10 for your time.

This study is purely anonymous and no personally identifying information will be asked for, nor kept about you.

tl;dr version:

who:you!

1) one online questionnaires about how you use social media

  • ~15 mins for questionnaire,
  • ~45 min for interview
